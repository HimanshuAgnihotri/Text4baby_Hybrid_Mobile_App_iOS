Baby's Development

  • Baby at 11 Months

Baby might be very shy or even fearful with strangers and might cry when you or dad leaves. This is normal. Help baby feel secure with reassuring touches, eye contact, and words. Tell baby when you are leaving and say "bye-bye." Leaving without saying "goodbye" can make your baby upset and confused.
