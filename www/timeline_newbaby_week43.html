Baby's Development

  • Baby at 10 Months

Over the next few months, you'll find that baby has so much personality and has started to imitate you. Baby might be pretending to talk on the phone and is learning that his or her actions have an effect when they drop, dump, roll, push and pull toys. Your baby is growing more independent but will still want you right there when trying new things.
