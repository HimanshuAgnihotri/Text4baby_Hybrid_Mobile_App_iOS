Baby's Development

  • Baby at 12 Months

Baby can say things like "mama" or "dada." Help baby understand language and objects by using simple, clear words and phrases. Another great way to work with baby is, when possible, allow baby to choose between 2 options that you like. For example, "Do you want this green toy or this yellow toy?" Your baby loves talking and interacting with you.
