Baby's Development

  • Baby at 8 Months

As your baby learns to eat solid foods, start by offering 1 to 2 tablespoons, 2 to 3 times per day. Introduce one food at a time. It can take 10 to 15 exposures to a new food before baby learns to like it. Offer your baby mashed or pureed fruits and vegetables. You can even offer pureed red meat to baby, to give baby iron. Don't force baby to eat when full or make baby finish foods.
