Baby's Development

  • Baby at 3 Months

If baby is experiencing a lot of gas, there are a few things you can do to make baby more comfortable: lay your baby flat on his or her back and move baby's legs in a bicycling motion. Better yet - give baby some tummy time while baby is awake and you are in the room. Tummy time helps baby's head from becoming flat and strengthens baby's upper body. Tummy time also helps baby get rid of that uncomfortable gas.
