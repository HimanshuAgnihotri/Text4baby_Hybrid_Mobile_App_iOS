You probably have more pictures of baby than you can count! When you can, take time to organize them and create digital albums to share with friends and family. Print and frame some pictures for your home. Enjoy the memories - it goes by fast!

Baby's Development

  • Baby at 6 Months

Baby is starting to show a range of feelings like joy, surprise, anger, and fear. Play games with baby like peek-a-boo. Baby will love to discover finding you hidden behind your hands! Also talk to your baby as much as possible. Talking and singing help baby's brain get ready for talking by making patterns. Point at things that baby is looking at and talk about them. This will help make baby smarter.
