Baby's Development

  • Baby at 9 Months

Baby is now 9 months old! Baby can understand "no" and can make a lot of different sounds like "mamamama" and "babababa." Your baby should be able to copy sounds and gestures you make, and use fingers to point at things. Baby can stand with your support or the support of furniture. Baby can even pick up small things like cereal, between his or her thumb or index finger. Go baby!
