Introduction

The Service bring you general information on maternal and childhood health and runs for a period of up to 9 months during pregnancy and 12 months following birth. The Service take into account certain demographic information you provide along with your preferences, and is fine-tuned over time based on your progress.

This Privacy Policy outlines how we protect your Personally Identifiable Information, what Personally Identifiable Information we may collect, and how we may use that Information. This Policy does not cover our use of any Personally Identifiable Information collected through any means other than the text4baby Service.

We Respect Your Privacy

The Service Providers, our Partners and all of our affiliates respect your privacy and that of every individual who visits or signs up for the Service, or provides us with "Personally Identifiable Information" (or "PII") via the interactive features of the Service or contacts us through our Website by e-mail. "Personally Identifiable Information" means any information (i) that identifies or can be used to identify, contact, or locate the person to whom such information pertains, or (ii) from which identification or contact information of an individual person can be derived. Personally Identifiable Information includes, but is not limited to: first and last name, address, phone number, email address or Health Insurance Plan ID. In providing the Service, we both receive PII from you, and send PII to you. We may also receive your PII from our Partners if you have given them permission to share it with us.

We do not sell, rent, or lease your Personally Identifiable Information to any third party.

Where it is necessary to share Personally Identifiable Information we will either request the individuals explicit consent or, for example to support the formal evaluation of our Service, only do so with third parties that have been authorized and vetted by us in advance and who have signed a Data Use Agreement (or similar) that hold them responsible for protecting the information shared in line with this Privacy Policy. If you provide your consent your contact information will only be used by us or by one of our authorized third parties. If you do not provide your consent you will not be contacted.

You do not have to permit cookies to use our Website, although certain features of our Website may not be available to you without cookies.
