Baby's Development

  • Baby at 2 Months

Babies need to be exposed to a variety of textures throughout the day, and tummy time is the perfect opportunity to accomplish this. When your baby is on his or her tummy, the skin on your baby's stomach, legs, arms, and face touches the surface on which your baby is lying. The most natural place to play is on a clean floor, a nap mat, or blankets of different textures.
