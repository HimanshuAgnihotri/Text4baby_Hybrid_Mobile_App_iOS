Baby's Development

  • Baby at 12 Months

Happy Birthday!!! At one year, baby's vision is now fully developed and baby is much more advanced! You'll notice that baby is able to find hidden things easily and even understands things. For example, baby can look at the right picture when it is named and understand simple directions like picking up a toy. Baby should be able to respond to simple spoken requests by shaking his or her head "no" or waving "bye-bye." Baby has grown so much and loves to explore!
