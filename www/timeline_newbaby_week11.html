Baby's Development

  • Baby at 2 Months

Avoid overfeeding your baby because this might make baby uncomfortable. Try to wait at least 2 to 2.5 hours from the beginning of one feeding to the beginning of the next. Baby will give you signs if he or she is still hungry after a feeding like crying or smacking lips after eating. Your doctor will help you keep track of baby's development. Generally from months 1 to 4 baby should gain about 1.5 to 2 pounds each month.
