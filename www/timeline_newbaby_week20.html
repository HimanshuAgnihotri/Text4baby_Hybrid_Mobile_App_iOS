Baby's Development

  • Baby at 4 Months

Your baby will gain strength and flexibility by doing tummy time while awake and you are in the room. Distract your baby with a fun toy or read your baby a book during tummy time. Baby likes lots of positions throughout the day, including spending time on your lap and in your arms. This helps your baby feel connected to you and your partner. Talking and reading to baby will help baby's brain develop and help baby talk.
