Baby's Development

  • Baby at 4 Months

Baby can now follow things with his or her eyes from side to side. Talk to your doctor if your baby doesn't watch things as they move, smile at people, hold head steady, make cooing sounds, or bring things to mouth. If baby seems to have trouble moving his or her eyes in one or both directions, baby's sight should be checked. If you are worried about baby's sight, talk to baby's Dr.
