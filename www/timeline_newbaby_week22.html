Baby's Development

  • Baby at 5 Months

Baby is starting to babble and make sounds, using many of the sounds of language. Although it may sound like gibberish, if you listen closely, you'll hear your baby's voice rise and drop as if making a statement or asking a question. Encourage your baby by talking to him or her throughout the day. When baby says a "oooh" or "aaaah," repeat it back and then say some simple words that have that sound.
