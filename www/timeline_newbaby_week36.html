Baby's Development

  • Baby at 8 Months

Baby is now 8 months old! By the time baby reaches his eighth month, he probably will weigh about two and a half times what he did at birth. Baby's bones will continue to grow at a rapid rate. As a result during these months, baby's length will increase by about 2 inches (5 cm) and baby's head circumference by about 1 inch (2.5 cm).
