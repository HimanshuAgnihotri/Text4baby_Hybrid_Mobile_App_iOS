Baby's Development

  • Baby at 9 Months

Babies usually start to crawl between 7 and 10 months. You can help with baby's development by putting toys outside of babies reach to encourage crawling and by supporting baby in standing or even trying to walk. Baby should be able to sit without your support and should respond to his or her name when you say it. Contact baby's Dr. if you have concerns about baby's development.
