There are a lot of things to remember when you’re pregnant or a new mom. Text4baby makes it easy to get important information – and it’s free.

Text4baby moms receive health and safety information through the app and text messages to help through pregnancy and baby’s first year.

The text messages are personalized to your due date or baby’s birth date, and sent three times per week addressing topics such as labor signs and symptoms, prenatal care, nutrition, baby’s development, urgent health alerts and product recalls, immunizations, safety, and more. Text STOP to discontinue messages or HELP for help at any time.

The app offers additional information each week of your pregnancy through baby’s first year.

You can:

  • Track your progress

  • See the size of your baby each week

  • Set up appointment reminders

  • Take fun quizzes to see how you compare to other moms

  • And much more!

The Text4baby app and text messages combined give you the most critical information to help prepare you for motherhood. The content is created in collaboration with the top maternal and child health experts, so it’s information you can trust.

Text4baby is the largest national mobile health initiative reaching over 725,000 moms since 2010.
