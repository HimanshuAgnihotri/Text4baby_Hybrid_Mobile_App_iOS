Did you have diabetes during pregnancy ("gestational diabetes")? Sometimes women with gestational diabetes will have type 1 or type 2 diabetes after the baby is born. If so, you will need medicine to keep your blood sugar in a healthy range. Ask your provider about getting tested.

Baby's Development

  • Baby at 7 Months

You can help with your baby's development by repeating the sounds your child makes and make simple words with those sounds. For example, if your child says "bah", say "bottle" or "book." Praise baby when he or she babbles. Also read books to your baby everyday. Reading together helps baby develop the patterns for language development.
