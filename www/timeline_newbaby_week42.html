Make a plan to snuggle with your partner. Many new parents say that romance fades with day-to-day family life, but the first step is to talk about it. Then schedule a date night or special activity. Or look up ideas for things to do at home (after baby is asleep).

Baby's Development

  • Baby at 10 Months

Keep using "no" with baby for things that put him or her in danger. Even after you tell baby "no," baby may try to touch or do it again. Your baby does this to learn "no" and to test boundaries. Stay firm and say no again. Keep the word "no" for things that can really hurt baby. Your baby is learning so much!
