Folic Acid

Take a prenatal vitamin each day that has 600 micrograms of folic acid in it. This may help reduce baby's risk for birth defects of the brain and spine called neural tube defects (NTDs). The neural tube is the part of a developing baby that becomes the brain and spinal cord. Your health care provider can prescribe a prenatal vitamin for you. You can also get prenatal vitamins over the counter without a prescription.
