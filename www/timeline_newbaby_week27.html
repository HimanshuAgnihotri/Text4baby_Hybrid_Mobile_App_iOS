Baby's Development

  • Baby at 6 Months

Teething can start from 4 to 7 months. Teething can make baby fussy, irritable, drool, and cry. Sometimes baby will get a low temperature (a low temperature is under 101 degrees Fahrenheit or 38.3 degrees Celsius.) To help baby, try gently rubbing or massaging the gums with one of your fingers. Teething rings are helpful, too, but they should be made of firm rubber.
