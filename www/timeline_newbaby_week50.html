Having a baby is stressful, but if you feel very sad, anxious, angry, or have negative emotions about baby, please reach out for help. Postpartum depression can start early or set in when baby is older. You are not alone; professionals are available for free. Call 800-944-4773.

Don't forget to schedule baby's 1 year check-up and baby's first dentist appointment. Brushing baby's teeth twice a day with plain water and a soft toothbrush is important for baby's teeth health. Talk to your dentist about a flouride supplement for baby and tips to keep baby's mouth clean.
