Baby's Development

  • Baby at 11 Months

Baby is starting to say "mama" or "dada." Praise baby's efforts in talking and gesturing with your words. For example, if baby is excited about a ball and gestures at it, support baby by saying "yes, that's a blue ball." This will help build baby's vocabulary and understanding of the objects around him or her.
