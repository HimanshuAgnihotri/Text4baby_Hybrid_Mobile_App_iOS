Baby's Development

  • Baby at 6 Months

Babies learn fast, and before you know it, your baby will be wiggling off a bed or reaching for your cup of hot coffee. As your baby grows and is able to roll over, he or she may fall off of things unless protected. Do not leave your baby alone on changing tables, beds, sofas, or chairs. Put your baby in a safe place such as a crib or playpen when you cannot hold him or her.
