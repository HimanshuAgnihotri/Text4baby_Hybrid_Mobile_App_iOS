Mom this Week

Welcome to your second trimester! Hopefully you have energy and feel less sick now, but might have new back or stomach pain, leg cramps, constipation, or heartburn. Talk to your partner, friends and family about what you're going through!

How Big is Baby?

Your baby is the size of a passionfruit!

This week baby's organs are starting to work. Baby can now make urine and white blood cells (these will help baby stay healthy after she's born). Baby's taste buds have also formed.
